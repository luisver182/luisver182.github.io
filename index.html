My Learning Experience in Python and HTML

At the beginning of this online course, learning Python and HTML felt easy and even exciting. The first lessons were simple to follow, and I felt motivated to dive deeper into programming.

However, as the weeks went by, the course became much more difficult and intensive. Some topics were very complex, and learning to code while studying online was sometimes challenging. It required a lot of focus, practice, and patience.

Despite the difficulties, I learned so much. I can now write basic programs in Python, understand loops, conditionals, and functions. I also gained the ability to structure a webpage using HTML. These are skills I never thought I would have, and I’m proud of the progress I made.

One of the biggest reasons I was able to succeed in this course was because of our amazing teacher, Ms. Brittany Smith. Even though the classes were online, she made the experience feel very engaging and supportive. Her way of explaining things was always clear, friendly, and easy to understand. She had a special way of making us feel confident, even when the material got tough. I truly appreciate her patience and dedication.

In conclusion, this online class was both challenging and rewarding. I’ve grown a lot, not just as a student, but also in my confidence with technology. I am sincerely thankful to Ms. Brittany Smith for her excellent teaching and the passion she shared with all of us.
